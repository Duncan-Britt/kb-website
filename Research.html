Research

Most of my current and past work is in epistemology. I'm currently interested in questions regarding the nature of evidence and evidential support, higher-order evidence, and the nature of epistemic norms. While I don't have any current work that I'm ready to share right now, I'll be posting drafts on this site once my work is ready.


My MA thesis was on the normativity of logic. There, I argued, contra some philosophers, that logic is normative for both belief and for reasoning. I then explored some of the consequences of that. For those interested, the thesis is posted below.
