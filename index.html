About Me

I'm a graduate student in philosophy at Princeton University. Before coming to Princeton, I did an MA in philosophy at the University of Colorado, Boulder, where I also did my BA in philosophy and linguistics. My main areas of interest are in epistemology, philosophy of language, and formal semantics.
